Explore Cape Town — Top Experiences

A curated list of must-do activities across city, coast, and winelands

Table Mountain

Take the cable car or hike to the summit for magnificent views.

Bo-Kaap

Stroll colorful streets and learn about Cape Malay heritage.

V&A Waterfront

Shop, dine, and explore the harbor — perfect for sunset strolls.

Sunset Cruise

Sail from the Waterfront on a yacht or boat at golden hour.

Two Oceans Aquarium

Discover marine life where the Atlantic meets the Indian Ocean.

Kirstenbosch Gardens

Picnic, walk, and photograph South Africa’s famed botanical garden.

Boulders Beach Penguins

Meet friendly penguins at Simon’s Town — a perfect family stop.

Cape Point Reserve

Stand at the southern tip with dramatic cliffs and ocean views.

Chapman’s Peak Drive

Scenic coastal drive via Hout Bay and Noordhoek — breathtaking photo stops.

Constantia Valley

Wine tasting at the oldest wine farms near Cape Town.

Paarl & Wellington

Explore rich winelands with heritage and scenic valleys.

Worcester & Tulbagh

Discover wine country towns with Cape Dutch architecture.

Franschhoek Tram

Hop-on, hop-off wine tasting with French heritage in a beautiful valley.

Durbanville Wine Region

Famous for Sauvignon Blanc — enjoy pairings and platters.

Clifton Beach

Relax on Cape Town’s iconic beach — perfect for a beach day.

Seal Island – Hout Bay

Take a boat trip from the harbor to meet playful seals.

Kalk Bay

Best fish & chips, quirky shops, and a charming harbor walk.

Lion’s Head & Signal Hill

Hike Lion’s Head or drive Signal Hill for panoramic views.

Atlantis Dunes & Paragliding

Quad biking on dunes and paragliding off Signal Hill.

Clarence Drive

Scenic drive from Gordon’s Bay to Rooi-Els along the coast.

Boulders Beach at Sunset

Capture penguins with Table Mountain backdrop at golden hour.

Sir Lowry’s Pass to Hermanus

Admire views and spot whales in season from lookouts near Hermanus.

Du Toitskloof to Robertson & Montagu

Mountain pass vistas and renowned Chenin Blanc in the fruit region.

Stellenbosch, Franschhoek & Babylonstoren

Iconic wine tours across famed estates and garden farm experiences.
