Our Story

Welcome to Tour Da Cape, where the Mother City's most extraordinary experiences await you. Born from a deep love for Cape Town's unique blend of cultures, landscapes, and stories, we are passionate locals who have dedicated our lives to sharing the authentic soul of this remarkable city.

Founded in 2019 by Cape Town natives who grew up exploring Table Mountain's hidden trails, surfing the Atlantic swells, and discovering the rich heritage of Bo-Kaap and District Six, our team brings generations of local knowledge to every tour we offer. We recognized that visitors deserved more than surface-level experiences – they deserved to see Cape Town through the eyes of those who call it home.

What started as weekend adventures with friends has grown into Cape Town's most trusted boutique tour operator, serving discerning travelers who seek authentic connections with our city's natural wonders, cultural heritage, and vibrant communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reveal the true essence of Cape Town – from the ancient Khoi-San heritage to the modern Rainbow Nation spirit – through immersive experiences that honor our city's complex history while celebrating its bright future.

We believe every visitor should experience the magic of a Table Mountain sunrise, taste wine where it was first cultivated in Africa, walk in Nelson Mandela's footsteps on Robben Island, and feel the rhythm of township jazz. Our carefully crafted journeys create transformative moments that connect hearts across cultures.

We are committed to responsible tourism that empowers local communities, from Bo-Kaap spice merchants to Stellenbosch winemakers, ensuring that tourism becomes a force for positive change in the Mother City.

Meet Our Team

The passionate professionals behind your unforgettable experiences

James Robertson

Founder & Heritage Guide

A third-generation Capetonian with deep roots in the city's maritime history. James specializes in Robben Island tours and Cape Peninsula adventures, bringing 15 years of guiding experience and a Master's in South African History.

Sarah Caine

Cultural Experience Director

Born in Bo-Kaap, Sarah is fluent in Afrikaans, English, and Xhosa. A former travel journalist, she curates our township and cultural tours, ensuring authentic community connections and meaningful cultural exchange.

Michael Du Plessis

Wine & Nature Specialist

A certified sommelier and qualified mountain guide, Michael leads our Winelands expeditions and Table Mountain adventures. His family has been making wine in Stellenbosch for four generations.
